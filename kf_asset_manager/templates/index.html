Scan an asset folder

Point to any folder of artwork — sets, patterns, pieces, in any layout. Nothing is renamed or moved. Identity is the file's content, so re-scanning after you reorganise keeps every ID and every manual edit.

After scanning you'll review every asset, edit anything (your edits are permanent overrides), approve, then export the manifest and crosswalk.
